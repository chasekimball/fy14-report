Kimball Campbell Report

Fiscal Year 2014

The federal fiscal year began with a shutdown. For the Nation, it was an embarrassment and a hazard to our stability. But for Heather and Chase--two federal employees who had been very busy and eager for some quiet time together--the forced furlough came as a welcome reprieve. On October 1, the first day of the Shutdown, we escaped the city for the hills of West Virginia and a discussion about life, love, and our future. Two days later, after a very long walk, Heather and Chase proposed to each other on the banks of the Potomac River. They were married in Albuquerque, New Mexico on March 15, 2014.

The alignment of the new fiscal year with the beginning of Heather and Chase's new betrothed relationship inspired this Report. The Kimball Campbell FY 2014 Report documents their relationship and their activity in general, based on a variety of datasets. They travel extensively, enjoy dinner with friends, and communicate often.
